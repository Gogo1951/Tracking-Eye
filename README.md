# Tracking Eye

A smart tracking menu for World of Warcraft Classic and Anniversary Edition that auto-cycles herb and ore tracking while mounted, and automatically restores your tracking ability after death.

## Features

**Tracking Menu** — Left-click the minimap button to see all your tracking abilities in one place. Select a spell to set it as your persistent tracking ability.

**Persistent Tracking** — Automatically recasts your chosen tracking spell after you resurrect. No more forgetting to re-enable Find Herbs after a corpse run.

**Farm Mode** — While mounted or in travel form, cycles between your selected tracking abilities (Herbs and Minerals by default) on a configurable timer. Automatically pauses in capital cities and battlegrounds so it only runs where it matters.

**Free Placement Mode** — Detach the tracking icon from the minimap and place it anywhere on your screen. Choose between circle and square border styles, and scale the icon to your preference.

## Supported Classes

- **Hunters** — All tracking spells (Beasts, Demons, Dragonkin, Elementals, Giants, Hidden, Humanoids, Undead)
- **Druids** — Track Humanoids (Cat Form only)
- **Warlocks** — Sense Demons
- **Paladins** — Sense Undead
- **All classes** — Find Herbs, Find Minerals, Find Treasure, Find Fish

## Installation

Install from [CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/tracking-eye-classic) using the CurseForge app, or download the latest release and extract it into your `Interface/AddOns` folder.

## Usage

### Minimap Button Controls

| Action | Effect |
|---|---|
| Left-Click | Open tracking menu |
| Right-Click | Clear current tracking |
| Shift + Left-Click | Toggle Persistent Tracking |
| Shift + Right-Click | Toggle Farm Mode |
| Shift + Middle-Click | Toggle Free Placement Mode |

### Slash Commands

`/te` or `/trackingeye` — Open the options panel.

### Options Panel

Access additional settings under **Options > AddOns > Tracking Eye**, including:

- Enable/disable Persistent Tracking and Farm Mode independently
- Choose which abilities Farm Mode cycles through
- Adjust the cycle speed (2–10 seconds, default 3.5)
- Enable Free Placement Mode with adjustable icon size and shape
- Reset all options to defaults
